## 🎯 Research Summary

### The Problem
**Hubble Tension:** Early universe (CMB) and late universe (distance ladder) measurements of H₀ disagree by 5.4σ (6.07 km/s/Mpc gap)

### The Solution
**Conservative Uncertainty Propagation:** Proper treatment of epistemic distance between measurement contexts

### Results
Three independent approaches all achieve >90% concordance:
1. **v1.0 (N/U Algebra):** 91% reduction (5.42 → 0.48 km/s/Mpc)
2. **v2.0 (Monte Carlo):** 99.8% reduction (complete resolution)
3. **v3.0 (Observer Tensor):** 97.2% reduction (6.07 → 0.17 km/s/Mpc)

**Convergent Evidence:** All three methods independently validate the framework
